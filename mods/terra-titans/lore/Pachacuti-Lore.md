# Pachacuti — Earth-Shaker

> Ruler who expanded the Inca Empire  
> Alignment: Engineering / Expansion / Construction

## Background

Pachacuti transformed a regional kingdom into the vast Inca Empire in the fifteenth century. He is credited with reorganizing the state and directing extraordinary feats of engineering: terraced agriculture across steep mountainsides, an immense road network, and monumental stone architecture.

The famed mountain estate of Machu Picchu is associated with his reign — a testament to Andean mastery of building in the most demanding terrain.

## Board Persona

On the board, Pachacuti builds where others cannot. Reduced upgrade costs reflect Andean engineering skill, letting him develop deeply and economically.

## Passive — Terraced Engineering

## Style

Pachacuti builds by three convictions:

- No terrain is too steep to terrace
- Roads and stone are the bones of an empire
- Build efficiently, and build to last

He turns difficult ground into developed value, raising more for less on every plot.
